Purpose

Welcome to Carbon Paw Print! This website allows users to calculate their carbon footprint based on appliances they use and their car details. Carbon Paw Print then provides local resources and other suggestions to decrease your emissions. Our goal is to help users better understand the effects of their carbon footprint on the world!
